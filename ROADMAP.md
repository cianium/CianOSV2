# CianOS Roadmap

## Vision

Build a Life RPG Operating System that transforms real-life learning and productivity into an immersive progression experience.

# Long-Term Vision

CianOS aims to become a complete Life RPG platform where:

- Learning becomes progression
- Habits become quests
- Goals become boss fights
- Personal growth becomes a game

The user is the character.
Real life is the world.
